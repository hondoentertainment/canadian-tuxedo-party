The Dress Code

What is a Canadian Tuxedo, anyway?

A Canadian Tuxedo is the timeless art of wearing denim on denim — think jeans plus a denim jacket, shirt, or vest. It's bold, it's classic, and it's basically the unofficial uniform of this party.

  • Jeans + denim jacket = chef's kiss
  • Double denim in any shade — light, dark, or mixed
  • Denim accessories welcome: hats, belts, bandanas
  • No tux required — but bonus points for creativity
